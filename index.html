Mohbad in 2021

Here is a time-line of Mohbad's life:


  • 1996 - Ilerioluwa Olademeji Aloba was born June 8, 1996 in Ketu Lagos,Nigeria. He got his stage name Mohbad a.k.a Imole with respect to his music career

  • 2013 - In 2013, Ilerioluwa was enrolled in Abifab College Ikorodu Lagos where he passed his NECO examination at one sitting

  • 2015 - Ilerioluwa wrote a successful National Examination Council (NECO) in 2015 as a commercial class student

  • 2019 - In 2019, Ileri rose to stardom after he was recognized as a street artist and was signed into the Marlian's Record by Naira Marley

  • 2020 - He released his debut album Lightwhich features Davido, Lil Kesh and Naira Marley after his hit song "ko por ke(kpk)" and "Ponmo"

  • 2021 - He was nominated five times for The Beatz Awards Mohbad was listed in Audiomack's top 21 Afrobeat artist in 2021

  • 2022 - Mohbad left Marlian Records. He released Peace produced by Rexxie which became a major hit

  • 2023 - Mohbad released his first EP after leaving Marlians Record Blessed under his own label Imoleniztion.The eight songs on the 20-minute EP featured Nigerian artists Zlatan and Bella Shmurda, and producers Niphkeys and Timi Jay. The album charted at number one on Apple Music in Nigeria the week it was released, and subsequently, immediately after his death

  • 2023 - on Tuesday evening,September 12, 2023 Imole was confirmed dead. The cause of his death is yet to be known as Nigerians and his beloved fans awaits the autopsy result from the Nigerian Police Force.
